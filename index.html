Who We Are

We’re a local cybersecurity team focused on penetration testing, which is also known as ethical hacking. Our goal is simple: help small businesses uncover security risks before the bad guys do.

We know what it’s like to grow a business with limited resources. That’s why we offer practical, no-nonsense security solutions that fit your budget and protect what matters most.

Why Vulnerability Management Matters

For small businesses, vulnerability management is critical. Cyberattacks often target businesses like yours, not just large corporations. Small vulnerabilities can lead to serious risks like data breaches, website defacement, or stolen customer information.

That’s where we come in. We help you find and fix weaknesses before they become real problems, giving you peace of mind so you can focus on growing your business.

What We Do

Penetration testing simulates real-world cyberattacks to identify vulnerabilities in your website and digital assets. It’s a proactive health check for your online presence that uncovers weak points before someone else does.

Every business is unique, and pricing depends on many factors. We’ll review your site personally and recommend what’s best for you.
